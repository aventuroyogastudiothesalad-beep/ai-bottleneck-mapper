AI Bottleneck & Agent Mapper - Instructions

This tool is designed to support our transition to AI-driven workflows. By mapping internal inefficiencies (bottlenecks), we can identify where an **AI Agent** can take over repetitive or manual tasks.

1️⃣ Roles & Workflow

👤 User (You)

Report the bottleneck. Describe the problem, the manual impact, and provide examples of frequent questions. Set your Impact Score.

💻 Developer Team

Reviews all entries. They assign a technical Effort Score and check the "Reviewed" box to validate the data for the Priority Matrix.

2️⃣ Examples by Category

When adding an entry, please select the most appropriate category:

📩 Client Requests

Recurring questions from clients or repetitive data gathering (e.g., "Where is my certificate?").

🛠️ Manual Tool Work

Moving data between systems, manual Excel updates, or "human robot" copy-pasting.

🧠 Internal Knowledge

Searching for technical specs, protocols, or policies within our own documentation.

📁 Other

Any other repetitive action that doesn't fit the above but slows you down.

3️⃣ Scoring System

  • Impact Score (1-10): Set by the User.
    How much does this hinder your work? 10 = Major daily blocker.
  • Effort Score (1-10): Set by the Developer.
    How hard is it to build the AI solution? 1 = Quick Win.
💡 Default Effort: All new ideas start with a default Effort of 5. The technical team will adjust this after their review.

4️⃣ The AI Priority Matrix

The tool automatically calculates a Priority Score. Our goal is to focus on Low Effort / High Impact opportunities first.
