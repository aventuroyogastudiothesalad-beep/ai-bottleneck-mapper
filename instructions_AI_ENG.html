📘 AI Agent Mapper: User Guide

👥 Roles & Workflow

👤 Business User

Identify the "What". Document manual or repetitive tasks. Rate the Impact (1-10) based on how much time or frustration it causes.

💻 Developer

Identify the "How". Review submissions and assign an Effort Score (1-10). Validate the entry to finalize the Priority Score.

💡 What is a Bottleneck?

Anything that feels like "human robot" work. If it's a pattern, it can be an AI Agent.

Category The Manual Problem AI Opportunity
Data Entry Moving data from PDFs to Excel manually. Extraction Agent
FAQs Answering the same Slack/Email questions daily. Knowledge Agent
Drafting Writing standard "Welcome" emails or contracts. Content Agent

📊 Priority Matrix

The Priority Score is calculated as $Impact / Effort$.
🚀 Quick Wins = High Impact + Low Effort. These are our top priorities!
